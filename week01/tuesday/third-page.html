Welcome to My Exercise Plan!

Are you ready to get started on your fitness journey?
This simple exercise plan will help you build a foundation
for a healthier and more active lifestyle.

Day 1: Walking

Start with a 15-minute brisk walk around your neighborhood
or a nearby park. Focus on maintaining good posture and breathing
rhythmically.

Day 2: Stretching

Perform a 10-minute stretching routine to improve flexibility.
Include stretches for your neck, shoulders, arms, legs, and back.
